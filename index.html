Common Questions
Everything you need
to know.

How much does Residial cost?

+

Residial is priced per door per month — $2.50/door for 1–50 doors (Starter), $1.75/door for 51–500 doors (Pro), and $1.50/door for 500+ doors (Enterprise). There's a $49/month minimum on the Starter plan. No setup fees, no contracts, cancel anytime.

Can I pass the cost to my residents?

+

Yes — and most property managers do. Simply add a "Building Communication & Safety" fee of $2–$3/door/month to your resident leases. At $1.75/door, charging residents $2.50 covers your cost and puts money back in your pocket. We provide a lease addendum template to make this easy.

How fast are alerts delivered?

+

Alerts are sent simultaneously to all selected residents in under 10 seconds. Voice calls and SMS are placed at the same time — not one by one. A 100-resident building takes the same time as a 10-resident building.

Do residents need to download an app?

+

No. Residial reaches residents directly on their existing phone via voice call and SMS. There's nothing for residents to install, sign up for, or learn. As long as they have a phone number on file, they'll be reached.

Is Residial TCPA compliant?

+

Residial provides the technology — compliance is your responsibility as the property manager. You must obtain proper written consent from residents to receive automated calls and texts. We provide a lease addendum template that includes TCPA consent language. We recommend consulting an attorney in your state before sending automated messages.

How long does setup take?

+

Most property managers are up and running in under 5 minutes. Sign up, upload your resident list as a CSV or Excel file, and send your first alert. No technical skills required. Our dashboard walks you through every step.

What types of emergencies can I send alerts for?

+

Residial supports 10 emergency alert types: 🔥 Fire/Evacuation, 💧 Water Main Break, ⚡ Power Outage, 💨 Gas Leak, 🌊 Flooding, 🚽 Sewage Backup, 🌡️ HVAC Failure, 🔒 Security Incident, 🛗 Elevator Failure, and 😷 Air Quality/Mold. You can also send monthly rent payment reminders. AI generates a professional message for each type — you just review and send.

What happens after my free trial?

+

Your 14-day free trial includes full access to all features with no credit card required. Two days before your trial ends, we'll send you a reminder email. After the trial, you'll need a paid subscription to continue sending alerts. You can cancel at any time — no questions asked.

Does Residial support Spanish-speaking residents?

+

Yes. Residial supports bilingual alerts in English and Spanish. You can send English only, Spanish only, or both simultaneously. The Spanish voice uses a native-speaking AI voice (Lupe) and SMS messages are sent separately in each language.
