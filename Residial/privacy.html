Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026  ·  residial.net

Residial ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, company name, phone number, and the number of doors you manage.

Resident Data: You may upload resident information including names, unit numbers, and phone numbers. This data is used solely to send alerts on your behalf.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the Service, including alert history, message content, and delivery logs.

Payment Information: Payment details are processed by Stripe and are not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • Send emergency alerts and communications on your behalf
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Send transactional emails and service notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information or resident data to third parties.

3. Resident Data

You are the data controller for all resident information you upload. Residial acts as a data processor. We process resident data only as instructed by you and as necessary to provide the Service. You are responsible for ensuring that residents have consented to receive automated communications.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

  • Twilio: Our SMS and voice call provider, to deliver messages to residents
  • Stripe: Our payment processor, to handle billing
  • Render: Our hosting provider, for infrastructure
  • Law enforcement: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Alert history and logs are retained for 12 months. Resident data is retained until you delete it or close your account. Upon account termination, we will delete your data within 30 days upon request.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure cloud hosting, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Cookies

Residial uses minimal cookies necessary for the Service to function, including session cookies for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
