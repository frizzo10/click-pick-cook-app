Notification Consent

This page allows residents to consent to receiving emergency alerts and important notifications from their property management company via SMS text message and voice call.

What messages will I receive?

By opting in, you agree to receive the following types of messages from your property manager via Residial:

  • 🔥 Emergency alerts — fire, gas leak, flooding, evacuation notices
  • 💧 Building maintenance updates — water shutoffs, power outages, HVAC issues
  • 🔒 Security notifications — building security incidents or lockdown alerts
  • 📢 Important building notices — time-sensitive community announcements
  • 💰 Rent payment reminders — monthly payment due date reminders

Message frequency varies. You may receive urgent alerts at any time of day or night when a genuine emergency occurs.

Message & data rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Residial does not charge residents for receiving alerts. Contact your mobile carrier for details about your messaging plan.

To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any text message. To get help, reply HELP.
